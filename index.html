Students for the Exploration and Development of Space
Probation-2022

Sessions

Seds KCT Probation is a period of training for freshly recruited SEDSKCT members. Members will learn the fundamentals of engineering for space exploration throughout this time. The management phase and the technical phase of this probation have been separated. Members will be introduced to the management phase in order to strengthen their soft skills and team bonding. They will be introduced to the basic three areas of engineering, which are Design, Electronics and Communication, and Programming, during the technical phase.
